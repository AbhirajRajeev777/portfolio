I'm

Designer, Developer

I'm a ux /ui designer austin based who loves clean, simple & unique design. I also enjoy crafting brand identities, icons, & ilustration work.

My design approach is firmly rooted in empathy and understanding, placing users at the heart of every decision. I believe that by truly understanding the needs, behaviors, and motivations of users, we can create experiences that not only meet but exceed their expectations.

UI/UX DESIGN

Proficiency in both user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design.

Responsive Design

Ability to create designs that adapt seamlessly across various devices and screen sizes.

WEB DESIGN

Skills in designing engaging and user-friendly websites.

Wireframing

Proficiency in creating wireframes to visualize layout and structure before final design implementation

Prototyping

Expertise in creating interactive prototypes to demonstrate user flows and interactions.

Usability Testing

Experience in conducting usability tests to gather feedback and iterate on design solutions.
